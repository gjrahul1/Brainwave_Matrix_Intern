Romantic Thriller
A Thousand Deep Kisses

Humiliatingly rejected by Haasil, even after she thought she had him, Pallavi sets forth on a self-destructive path, seeking one life-thrill after the other. All she desires is to heal the wounds that haunt her every move, not allowing her to be herself. Neither can she forget Haasil nor can she reach him any more. That is, until she meets . . . Palki, Haasil’s ex-wife who is presumed dead by the world.

Review

Coming to the book, for sure it is a romantic thriller or we can say romantic mystery novel . And this will be a guarantee that each page of this book will keep the readers hooked from the very first page to the end of this book. I was dazzled to see the concept of the author where I can feel both the romance and the mystery moving parallel in this book.

The story line is not only vivid but is set in the wilderness of some uncanny characters who are pretty vile but readers will adore for sure. Pallavi is setting on her adventurous journey of making her life more beautiful and taking that serious revenge upon soon who has never showed any care to her except ignorance. That kiss took a U-turn in Pallavi’s life after which Haasil never appeared to be the love in hr life who will cup her all around into his arms.

She can’t bear the rejection and hence, that day comes when she encounters someone very close to Haasil and now creates the web of traps for him or may be some other plans to hurt Haasil in her own way. Pallavi may appear vicious or vengeful for the readers at some point of time but trust me there are lots more to know about her and her state of such doings. A roller coaster ride of emotional breakage and Swadha may have done nothing in Pallavi’s life except a very new arrival for her despondent life. What Pallavi does at the end to the very love of her life who she may never forget ? Will Haasil able to know about Palki? Any depicted point of cross-sections between our lovable characters?

The Best Couple Ever

Do you flaunt your happy moments in the form of filtered photographs on Facebook, Instagram, etc.? Do you and your partner set relationship goals for others on social media? Do you make people jealous of the perfect life you are living? Do you portray yourself as a forever-happy person to your social media followers? Do you think you are a cyber-world aspiration? If no, then chill. If yes, then congrats! You are their next target.

Review

Author has chosen a very apt topic for today's generation. Everyone will be able to relate to it and more than that they can even learn from it. But the author has not only written about social media but it also has corruption,true love, status of women in India. Even though it has so many topics it never gets overlapping. Each subject is very truly and beautifully connected in the story.

Story is told through female perspectives. Characters are very well developed. Each character has their own story to tell but you never feel it is too much. You can relate to each female characters. How often men in India take their respective partners for granted!

Plot of the story is very interesting. Graph of the story is very well maintained throughout the book. Every twist and turn just makes you more confused and more intrigued in the story. And I fell in love with the ending. I am generally very cynic when it comes to ending of a thriller book but the ending of this book did not disappoint me at all.

I am in love with this book. I have read Novoneel Chakraborty work before and his writing style is quite different from other Indian authors. If you are looking for some Indian thriller books you should definitely checkout his work. I recommend it to everyone.

Marry Me, Stranger

I'm Rivanah Bannerjee, a young and independent girl living alone in Mumbai. My parents love me, my boyfriend adores me and I have a great job. But heres the thing-my life is in danger. Someones been following me around, watching my every move, trying to get control over my life. At first I thought it was a silly prank to gain my attention. My roomie suggested he must be a secret admirer. Is he? What he doesnt know is the police have set a trap to nab him. Soon Ill know if its simply a lovers obsession or there is more to it. BTW, I call him stranger. From the bestselling author of EX, how about a sin tonight? That kiss in the rain and a thing beyond forever comes a racy tale gravid with emotional twists, relationship quirks and mind-numbing revelations.

Review

Marry Me, Stranger by Novoneel Chakraborty is an intriguing and gripping psychological thriller that keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the very end. The story follows Rivanah Bannerjee, a young woman who becomes entangled in a mysterious online game of truth and dare with a stranger who calls himself "Stranger." As the dares escalate, Rivanah's life takes a dark and dangerous turn, filled with secrets, suspense, and unexpected twists.

A stranger texts her, she replies back.. Someone harasses her and she doesn't report.. She is the typical example of a beautiful hot girl usually describe everywhere.. The independent image will clash with her. That's I guess interesting point because when we imagine a hard-working woman we specify her to certain traits.. The ones or norms we would follow.. What about a rebellion or who would play around the stranger game and won't apply the block, report option. What happens when we take such a step and how our life goes on a roller roster ride.. Chakraborty's writing style is immersive and keeps the readers hooked throughout the book. The character development is well-crafted, allowing readers to connect with the protagonist's emotional journey. The author skilfully weaves a web of suspense, leaving readers guessing the true identity of ’Stranger’ and the motives behind the game.

This book has got some kind of unknown thrills. I was so engrossed while reading it and couldn't stop untill completed and even after completion i continued thinking "what happens next?" . While the book effectively builds tension and explores the darker side of human nature, some readers might find the plot to be somewhat predictable in certain aspects. However, the engaging narrative compensates for any minor drawbacks, making "Marry Me, Stranger" a page-turner that mystery and thriller enthusiasts will enjoy. In conclusion, Marry Me, Stranger is a riveting novel that delves into the realms of obsession, trust, and deception. It is a worthwhile read for those seeking a gripping psychological thriller with a touch of romance and suspense.

And Like earlier segment , this book holds some realy-really-really-honest WISE WORDS , actally PRACTICAL WISE WORDS & like earlier book , this piece make me note many points in my notes sections .
